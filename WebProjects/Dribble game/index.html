HOW TO PLAY

Connect two NBA players through 6 or fewer mutual teammates.

Example: Connect Curry to LeBron
Stephen Curry
Kevin Durant
Kevin Love
Kyrie Irving
LeBron James

A valid guess since he played with Curry. He's never played with LeBron, so the game continues.
Guess 2: Kevin Love
An incorrect guess since he's never played with Durant.
Guess 3: Kyrie Irving
A valid guess since he's played with Durant, and also a winning guess since he played with LeBron.
